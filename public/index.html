How do I log a class? +
Go to the Home tab, fill in the date, location, and add one or more shifts using the + Add shift button. Each shift needs a class name, time, hours, and rate. Hit Submit when done.
What is the current pay period? +
Pay periods run bi-weekly (every two weeks). Your current period dates are shown in the dropdown at the top of the Home tab. You can switch to past periods to review or add missed entries.
Can I submit hours for a previous pay period? +
Yes — select a past pay period from the dropdown on the Home tab, enter your shifts, and submit. Prior period submissions are flagged as late and included in the next payroll report. You’ll see them listed separately on your Home tab.
How long after a pay period ends can I still submit on time? +
You have a 1-day grace period after the pay period ends to submit your hours on time. For example, if the pay period ends on March 15, you have until the end of the day on March 16 (midnight MST) to submit without it being marked late. Anything submitted on March 17 or later will be flagged as a late submission and included in the next payroll report.
How does the weekly shift bonus work? +
If you teach 6 or more hours in a single week, you earn a $5/hr bonus applied retroactively to all hours that week. The progress tracker on your Home tab shows how close you are each week.
What is the Pole 11+ bonus? +
When logging a pole class with 11 or more students, check the Pole 11+ checkbox on that shift. You’ll earn a $5 bonus on top of your hourly pay for that class.
How do I log a commission? +
On the Home tab there’s a dedicated Log Commission card. Enter the amount, date (defaults to today), and an optional note. Commissions appear separately from shifts in your entry list and pay summary.
I submitted wrong hours — can I fix it? +
Yes — on the Home tab, tap any entry to expand it and hit Edit. You can correct hours, rate, class name, or time. If the pay period has already been processed, contact admin directly.
Can I undo a delete? +
Yes — if you delete an entry by mistake, an Undo option appears briefly at the bottom of the screen. Tap it before it disappears to restore the entry.
What does it mean if my submission is not approved? +
Some staff require manual payroll approval. If your timesheet hasn’t been approved you’ll receive an email. Review your entries, make any corrections, and resubmit — your payroll admin is notified automatically when you do.
How do I view my pay history? +
Go to the Profile tab and scroll down to My Pay History. Choose a date range, hit Load Pay History, and you’ll see a full breakdown. Toggle between a period summary and a shift-by-shift view, or export as a PDF.
Can I download my pay statement for the current period? +
Yes — once you have entries logged, an ⬇ Export button appears in the top right of the This Pay Period card on Home, and also on the Reports tab. It opens a formatted statement you can print or save as PDF.
How do I change my email or PIN? +
Go to the Profile tab. You can update your email, set a username for login, and change your PIN. Changes take effect immediately.
Can I set a profile photo? +
Yes — go to the Profile tab, tap the pencil icon on the avatar, choose a photo, and hit Save photo.
How do I check my hours and pay at a glance? +
The summary bar on the Home tab shows your total shifts, commissions, hours, and gross pay for the selected period. Use the dropdown or the ‹/› arrow buttons to quickly switch between periods.
What’s on the Reports tab? +
The Reports tab shows a day-by-day breakdown of your logged shifts for the selected pay period, followed by weekly shift bonuses and a dedicated commissions section. Use the dropdown (with ‹/› buttons) to switch periods, and the Export PDF button to download a statement.
Will I get an email when payroll is sent? +
If your admin has enabled email reports for your account, yes — you’ll receive a pay breakdown when the payroll report is processed. Ask your admin if you’re unsure whether it’s turned on.
How do I import shifts from Google Calendar? +
First, connect your Google Calendar on the Profile tab. Then go to the Home tab and tap Import from Google Calendar (by pay period or by date). You’ll see your calendar events listed — toggle on the ones you want, set a bulk rate or per-event rate, and hit Import. The system auto-matches locations and detects duplicates.
Why does Google show an "unsafe" warning? +
When connecting Google Calendar, Google shows a warning that the app "hasn’t been verified." This is normal — it means we haven’t gone through Google’s lengthy app review process. Aradia only requests read-only access to your calendar event names, times, and locations. We never modify, delete, or share your data. To continue: click Advanced, then Go to Aradia Fitness (unsafe), then Continue.
Can I set a default Google Calendar? +
Yes — after connecting Google Calendar, go to the Profile tab. Below the connection status you’ll see a Default Calendar dropdown. Pick your preferred calendar and save. The import modal will automatically pre-select it so you don’t have to choose every time.
How does the calendar widget work? +
The calendar on the Home tab shows dots on any day with logged entries — even days outside the current pay period. Click a day to jump to that pay period, scroll to your entries, and highlight them. Click an empty future day to switch to that period and start logging. Use the toggle to switch between 2-week and month views.
What happens if I submit a duplicate entry? +
The system detects duplicates automatically. For Google imports, events matching an existing entry (same date + time) are flagged with a red badge and toggled off. For manual entries, you’ll get a confirmation prompt listing the duplicates before submitting. If another staff member has an entry at the same date, time, and location, you’ll see a "Heads up" warning (but can still submit).
Can I install this as an app on my phone? +
Yes — on iOS open the site in Safari, tap Share, and choose Add to Home Screen. On Android use Chrome and tap Add to Home Screen from the menu. You’ll get a full-screen app with no browser bar.
Why won't my Google Calendar connect? +
Google Calendar sync requires that the Gmail address on your Aradia account is registered as an approved test user with Google (our app is in testing mode while we go through Google's verification process). If you get an error when trying to connect, it usually means your email hasn't been added to the approved list yet. To fix this: use Message Support below and let us know the Gmail address you'd like to use for Google Calendar — we'll add it and let you know when it's ready. If you'd prefer to use a different Gmail account, just include that in your message.
How do I report a bug or ask a question? +
Scroll down on the Support tab. Use Report a Bug for anything broken, or Message Support for general questions. An admin will reply to your email.
