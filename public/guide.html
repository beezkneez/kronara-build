1. Getting Started

Kronara is a timesheet app built for the Kronara team. It runs entirely in your browser — no download required — and works on any phone, tablet, or computer.

Logging In

  1. Go to kronara.app in your browser.
  2. Enter your email address (or username, if you've set one) and your PIN.
  3. Tap Log In. You'll land on the Home page.
Tip Forgot your PIN? Tap Forgot PIN? on the login screen and we'll email it to you.

First-Time Setup

After your first login, head to the Profile tab to:

  • Set a username (so you can log in faster next time)
  • Upload a profile photo
  • Choose your preferred theme (Light, Dark, or Kronara)
  • Connect your Google Calendar (optional — makes importing shifts a breeze)

3. Logging a Class

This is the core of the app — here's how to log a shift step by step:

  1. Pick a date. Defaults to today. Tap the date field to choose a different day. The date must fall within the selected pay period (or you can switch periods first).
  2. Choose a location. Select from the dropdown — these are set up by your admin (e.g. "Downtown Studio", "Warehouse").
  3. Add optional notes. Anything you want your admin to see — "covered for Jess", "private lesson", etc.
  4. Fill in the shift details:
    Time (Start & End)
    Tap the time fields to open the clock picker. Drag the hand or type the time. AM/PM toggle included.
    Class / Party
    The name of the class you taught — e.g. "Pole 1", "Aerial Silks", "Kids Birthday Party".
    Hours Offered
    How many hours the shift was (e.g. 1.5). Auto-calculated from start/end times if provided.
    Hourly Rate
    Your pay rate for this class (e.g. 25). Check with your admin if unsure.
    Pole 11+ Bonus
    Check this box if you taught a pole class with 11 or more students. Adds a $5 bonus to that shift.
  5. Check the subtotal. The running total updates in real time as you fill in hours and rate.
  6. Tap Submit. Your entry is saved instantly and appears in the "This Pay Period" section below.
Duplicate Detection If another staff member already has an entry at the same date, time, and location, you'll see a "Heads up" warning. You can still submit — it's just a friendly check.

4. Multiple Shifts in One Day

Taught more than one class? No need to submit separately:

  1. After filling in your first shift, tap + Add shift at the bottom of the shift section.
  2. A new row appears. Fill in the time, class name, hours, and rate for the second class.
  3. Repeat for as many shifts as you need. Each line has its own delete button (X) if you change your mind.
  4. The subtotal updates to show the combined total of all shifts.
  5. Tap Submit once — all shifts are saved together under the same date.
Tip You can mix different locations and rates across shifts on the same day. Each shift is tracked independently.

5. Logging Commissions

Commissions (retail sales, tips, etc.) are logged separately from class shifts.

  1. Scroll down to the Log Commission card on the Home tab.
  2. Enter the dollar amount.
  3. Pick a date (defaults to today).
  4. Add an optional note (e.g. "retail sales", "private lesson tip").
  5. Tap Add Commission.

Commissions show up in your entry list with a 💰 icon and are counted separately in your pay summary. They appear in both the Home tab and the Reports tab under a dedicated "Commissions" section.

6. Editing & Deleting Entries

Editing an Entry

  1. Find the entry in the "This Pay Period" section on the Home tab.
  2. Tap the blue Edit button on that entry.
  3. The entry expands into an inline form where you can change: date, class name, location, start/end time, hours, rate, total, and notes.
  4. Make your changes and tap Save changes.
Changing the Date If you change an entry's date to a different pay period, the entry automatically moves to the correct period. You'll see a confirmation message and the view will switch to that period.

Deleting a Single Entry

Tap the red Delete button on any entry. It's removed immediately.

Deleting All Entries for a Day

Each day group has a "Delete day" button in the header. This removes all entries for that date at once.

Undo

After any delete, an Undo toast appears at the bottom of the screen for about 6 seconds. Tap it to restore what you just deleted.

Heads Up Once the undo timer runs out, the deletion is permanent. Make sure you meant it!

7. Pay Periods & Navigation

Everything in the app is organized around pay periods — the time window for which you log hours and get paid.

The Pay Period Selector

At the top of the Home tab, the dark strip shows:

  • A dropdown with all available pay periods (past, current, and future).
  • ‹ / › arrow buttons to quickly step backward or forward one period.
  • A stats bar showing total shifts, hours, and gross pay for the selected period.

Period Types

Your admin configures the pay schedule. It can be:

  • Biweekly — every 2 weeks (most common)
  • Weekly — every week
  • Semi-monthly — twice a month on fixed dates (e.g. 1st & 16th)
  • Monthly — once a month starting on a set day

Color Coding

  • Green border — you're viewing the current (active) pay period
  • Blue border — you're viewing a future period
  • No border — you're viewing a past period

8. The Calendar Widget

The calendar on the Home tab gives you a visual overview of your logged entries across days.

Two Views

  • Pay Period View (default) — shows only the days in the current pay period.
  • Month View — shows a full calendar month. Toggle with the Month View button.

Reading the Calendar

  • Days with entries have a tinted background and a small count badge showing the number of shifts.
  • Today is outlined with a red border.
  • Empty days are plain.

Interacting

  • Tap any day with entries to jump to that date's entries in the list below. If the day is in a different pay period, the app switches automatically.
  • Tap an empty day in a future period to switch to that period and start logging.
  • Use the ‹ / › arrows to navigate between periods (or months, in month view).
Tip The calendar syncs with the pay period dropdown. Switching periods via the dropdown also moves the calendar, and vice versa.

9. Bonuses & How They Work

Weekly Shift Bonus

If you teach 6 or more hours in a single week (Monday–Sunday), you earn a $5/hour bonus applied retroactively to all your hours that week.

  • A Weekly Bonus Tracker appears on the Home tab showing your progress toward the threshold.
  • Once you hit 6 hours, the bonus kicks in automatically — you don't need to do anything.
  • The bonus shows as a separate line in your reports.
Example You teach 7 hours in one week at $25/hr. Your base pay is $175. The weekly bonus adds $5 × 7 = $35, bringing your total to $210 for that week.

Pole 11+ Student Bonus

When logging a pole class with 11 or more students, check the Pole 11+ checkbox on that shift. This adds a flat $5 bonus on top of your hourly rate for that one class.

Bonus Exclusions

Some class types (like "Admin" or "Office Work") may be excluded from the weekly bonus threshold. These hours still get paid — they just don't count toward the 6-hour bonus target. Your admin configures which classes are excluded.

10. Late Submissions

A "late" entry is any shift logged after its pay period has already ended.

How It Looks

  • Late entries are highlighted with a light yellow background and a yellow left border.
  • A "Late" tag appears next to the class name.
  • This is visible on both the Home tab and the Reports tab.

How It Works

  • When you log a shift for a past pay period, it's automatically flagged as a late submission.
  • The entry goes into a pending submissions queue for your admin to review.
  • Late entries are included in the next payroll report automatically.

Fixing a Late Entry

If you edit a late entry and change its date to fall within the current pay period, the late tag is removed and the entry moves to the correct period. If you move it to a different past period, it stays marked as late but moves to the right place.

Good Habit Log your hours before the pay period ends! Late entries still get paid, but they require extra admin review and show up separately in payroll reports.

11. The Flag System

Flags are how your admin communicates that an entry needs attention — like incorrect hours, wrong rate, or missing info.

If Your Entry Gets Flagged

  1. An orange banner appears on your Home tab: "🚩 Flagged Entries" with a count.
  2. Tap the banner to see which entries were flagged and why (the admin's note).
  3. Tap "Respond to Flag" on any flagged entry.
  4. Write an explanation — e.g. "The hours are correct, it was a 2-hour private."
  5. Optionally, check "I'd like to submit corrected values" to propose new hours, rate, time, etc.
  6. Tap Submit Response. Your admin will review and resolve the flag.

Flag Statuses

  • Flagged — admin flagged it, waiting for your response
  • Corrected — you've submitted a response/correction
  • Resolved / Approved — admin reviewed and closed the flag
Tip Respond to flags promptly! Unresolved flags may carry over to the next pay period.

12. Google Calendar Integration

Connect your Google Calendar to import shifts directly — no manual entry needed.

Connecting

  1. Go to the Profile tab.
  2. Scroll to Google Calendar and tap Connect.
  3. Google shows an authorization screen. Click Advanced, then Go to Kronara (unsafe), then Continue.
  4. You'll be redirected back to the app. A "Connected ✓" badge appears.
  5. Select your Default Calendar from the dropdown and save it.
Why "Unsafe"? Google shows this warning because our app hasn't gone through their lengthy verification process. Kronara only requests read-only access to event names, times, and locations. We never modify, delete, or share your calendar data.

Importing Events

  1. On the Home tab, tap 📅 Import (in the calendar widget header) to import by period, or 📅 Import from Google Calendar (below the date field) to import by date.
  2. Your calendar events appear in a list. Toggle on the ones you want to import.
  3. Set a bulk rate ($/hr) that applies to all selected events, or tap individual events to set per-event rates.
  4. Tap Import Selected. Events become entries in your timesheet.

Smart Features

  • Location matching — the import automatically maps your calendar event locations to the app's location list.
  • Duplicate detection — events that match existing entries are flagged with a red badge and toggled off by default.
  • Default calendar — set once in Profile and never have to pick it again.

13. Reports & Exports

The Reports Tab

The Reports tab shows a professional breakdown of your pay for any period:

  • Daily breakdown — each day lists your shifts with location, time, hours, rate, and pay.
  • Weekly bonuses — if you hit the bonus threshold, it shows which weeks and how much.
  • Commissions — a separate section listing all commissions with dates, amounts, and notes.
  • Grand total — total shifts, hours, gross pay, and GST (if applicable).
  • Late entries appear with the yellow "Late" tag so you know which ones were submitted after the period closed.

Exporting

  • PDF export — tap the ⬇ Export PDF button on the Reports tab (or the export button on the Home tab). Opens a formatted, printable statement.
  • Pay History PDF — go to Profile → My Pay History, set a date range, and tap 🖨 Print/PDF for a comprehensive earnings statement.
  • Email attachments — in Profile → Preferences, toggle on Attach PDF and/or Attach CSV to receive formatted reports with your pay summary emails.

14. Your Profile

The Profile tab is your personal settings hub. Here's everything you can manage:

Profile Photo

Tap the pencil icon on your avatar to upload a photo. It shows in the top bar when you're logged in.

Email & Username

  • Change email — updates your login email.
  • Set a username — use it instead of your email to log in. Letters, numbers, dots, and dashes only.

Change PIN

Enter a new PIN (4–10 digits) and save. Takes effect immediately.

Contractor Settings

If you're set up as a contractor, you can:

  • Enter your GST number.
  • Toggle Charge GST to add 5% GST to all your pay in reports.

Preferences

  • Theme — choose Light, Dark, or Kronara.
  • Email me my pay summary — get an email when payroll is processed.
  • Attach PDF / CSV — get formatted files attached to your pay email.
  • Email me bug report updates — get notified when a bug you reported is fixed.

My Pay History

View all your earnings over any date range. Set start and end dates, tap Load Pay History, and see:

  • Total hours, gross pay, GST (if applicable), and total owing.
  • Toggle between Summary (by period) and Shifts (every individual entry) views.
  • Export with the 🖨 Print/PDF button.

17. Installing as a Phone App

You can add Kronara to your phone's home screen for a full-screen app experience — no browser bar, just the app.

iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open kronara.app in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add. The app icon appears on your home screen.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open kronara.app in Chrome.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right).
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen (or Install App).
  4. Tap Add. The app icon appears on your home screen.

18. Admin Features

If you're an admin or moderator, you'll see an extra Admin tab with powerful management tools.

Staff Management

  • View all staff members, their types (Employee/Contractor), and status.
  • Add new staff with email, name, and user type.
  • Edit or remove existing staff.

Payroll Processing

  • View per-period summaries: staff count, total hours, gross pay.
  • Send payroll reports to configured recipients (accountant, admin emails).
  • Review and manage pending (late) submissions before including in payroll.

Pending Submissions Tab

  • All late entries grouped by staff member and period.
  • Flag entries that need correction.
  • Exclude entries from payroll (uncheck the approval box).

Flagged Entries Tab

  • See all flagged entries across staff — grouped by status.
  • Read staff responses and proposed corrections.
  • Approve corrections or resolve flags with notes.

Settings

The admin settings panel lets you configure:

  • Report recipients — accountant email, admin emails, payroll admin.
  • Auto-send payroll — set a time (MST) for automatic report delivery at end of period.
  • Bonus configuration — weekly bonus threshold & amount, pole bonus amount, exclusion list.
  • Pay period settings — frequency, anchor dates, history count.
  • Locations — add, edit, or remove work locations.

19. Tips & Tricks

Log as you go Don't wait until end of period. Log each class right after you teach it — the date defaults to today so it takes 30 seconds.
Use Google Calendar import If your schedule is already on Google Calendar, connect it once and import shifts in two taps. The app auto-fills times, class names, and locations.
Check the bonus tracker Keep an eye on the weekly bonus progress bar on the Home tab. If you're close to 6 hours, one more class could unlock the $5/hr bonus for the entire week.
Use the calendar widget Quickly scan which days you've already logged by looking at the count badges. Tap any day to jump straight to those entries.
Set up email reports Go to Profile → Preferences and turn on Email me my pay summary. You'll get a detailed breakdown delivered to your inbox when payroll is processed — no need to check the app.
Bookmark the app Add Kronara to your phone's home screen (see section 17) for quick one-tap access without opening a browser.
