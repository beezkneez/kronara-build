Security & Privacy

Your studio’s data — and your members’ personal and payment information — deserve serious protection. Here’s how Kronara keeps it safe.

🔒 Protected by enterprise-grade security
🔐

Encrypted connections

Every page and request uses secure HTTPS/TLS encryption, so information is protected in transit — the padlock in your browser is real and verified.

🛡️

Firewall & attack protection

Kronara sits behind Cloudflare — the same network that protects millions of major websites — with an active web application firewall and DDoS protection running around the clock.

🤖

Bot & abuse defense

Automated bot-filtering blocks malicious scrapers and abusive traffic before it ever reaches the app.

🕶️

Hidden, hardened servers

Our application servers sit behind Cloudflare, so their real location is shielded and can’t be targeted directly.

🔑

Secure account access

Accounts are protected by personal PINs, sessions expire automatically, and access to studio data is limited by role.

📧

Authenticated email

Our emails are cryptographically signed (SPF, DKIM & DMARC) so you can trust messages that come from us — and scammers can’t impersonate us.

💳 Secure payments — powered by Stripe

All payments on Kronara are processed by Stripe, one of the world’s most trusted payment providers, used by millions of businesses globally.

This means your card details go directly and securely to Stripe — Kronara never sees or stores your full card number. Stripe is certified to the highest industry standard for handling card data (PCI-DSS Level 1), so every transaction is protected by bank-grade security.

Reliable & always-on

Kronara runs on a global network that keeps the app fast and available wherever you and your members are — with protection that automatically adapts to new threats.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how we protect your data, please use the Support tab within the app or contact us at admin@kronara.app. For details on what we collect and how it’s used, see our Privacy Policy.

Kronara uses industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections, a web application firewall, DDoS protection, and PCI-DSS-compliant payment processing via Stripe.
