Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Aradia Fitness ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Aradia Time application at aradiafitness.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Aradia Time:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, and PIN (stored securely).
  • Shift and Pay Data: Dates, hours, locations, class names, rates, and commission entries you submit.
  • Google Calendar Data: If you choose to connect your Google account, we access your calendar events solely to import shift data into Aradia Time. We only read event titles, times, and locations from your calendar. We do not modify, delete, or share your Google Calendar data.
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, theme preferences, and email notification settings.
  • Support Data: Messages and bug reports you submit through the Support tab.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Aradia Time shift tracking service.
  • To calculate and display your pay summaries and reports.
  • To send payroll reports and notifications (if you opt in to email notifications).
  • To import shift data from Google Calendar when you authorize the connection.
  • To respond to support requests and improve the application.

3. Google Calendar Integration

Aradia Time's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only request read-only access to your Google Calendar events.
  • Calendar data is used exclusively to match and import shifts into Aradia Time based on location.
  • We do not store raw Google Calendar data beyond what is needed to create shift entries.
  • We do not share, sell, or transfer your Google Calendar data to any third party.
  • You can disconnect your Google account at any time from the Profile tab.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data may be shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your employer/admin: Shift entries and pay summaries are visible to admin and moderator accounts for payroll processing.
  • Email delivery: We use third-party email services (Resend) to send payroll reports and notifications.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure database storage, and access controls. PINs are used for authentication and sessions expire automatically.

6. Data Retention

Your shift and pay data is retained for as long as your account is active. Archived staff accounts retain historical data for payroll record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting your administrator.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and view all your submitted shift and pay data.
  • Update your account information (email, username, PIN) at any time.
  • Disconnect your Google Calendar integration at any time.
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting your administrator.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the Support tab within the app or contact us at support@aradiafitness.app.
